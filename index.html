About the Club

Built on Innovation.
Driven by Collaboration.

The Creovators Club at Presidency University fosters creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship — equipping students with the skills and mindset to solve real-world problems through cutting-edge technology.

🔭

Vision

To establish a vibrant community of innovators, makers, and thinkers, driving technological advancement and contributing to societal progress.

🎯

Mission

To provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities for members to develop innovative solutions — promoting entrepreneurship, creativity, and collaboration.

⚡

Objective

Provide a collaborative platform to ideate, design, and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems using cutting-edge technologies.
